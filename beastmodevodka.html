Beast Mode Vodka

Description

This exotic vodka drink was fashioned to celebrate the Seattle Seahawks, their team colors, and their awe-inspiring running back.

Whether you're watching the game at home or tailgating outside the stadium, this cocktail will help you get into Beast Mode and roar like a real 12th Man!

Give it a Beast Mode quake, serve over ice, or shake into your favorite vodka-based cocktail. Garnish with a 12th Man flag!

Ingredients

  • 12 fluid ounces vodka

  • ½ cup blue or green fruit-flavored candies (such as Skittles®)

Steps

  1. Combine vodka and candies in a jar with a lid or cocktail shaker.

  2. Cover and shake until the colored candy coating has dissolved, about 10 seconds.

  3. Strain into a resealable bottle.

That's it! You've made it! :)
